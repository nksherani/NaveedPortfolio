# Categorized Skills: AI Solution Architect

This document outlines a targeted, categorized list of core skills mapped specifically to the requirements of the AI Solution Architect role. It highlights over 9 years of technology experience, 7 years of enterprise Azure cloud services, and 1.5+ years of hands-on Generative AI, MLOps, and Data Science engineering.

---

### 1. Generative AI, LLMs & Agentic AI
* **Agentic AI Workflows:** Hands-on experience designing and building multi-agent systems using **LangGraph** (specifically implementing cooperative Content, Engagement, Strategy, and Optimization agents) with brand-safety guardrails.
* **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation):** Expert in designing enterprise RAG architectures using **OpenAI embedding models**, specialized context-preserving chunking strategies, and custom high-fidelity page/clause citation mapping systems.
* **Local & Cloud LLMs:** Experience hosting, orchestrating, and prompting Local LLMs and commercial APIs (GPT-4, Gemini, GLM) for text generation, semantic reasoning, and database interfacing.
* **Prompt Engineering & Tool Calling:** Designing structured multi-step prompts (single-prompt categorizations, binary cascades, and hybrid majority-voting workflows) and integrating native model tool calling.

### 2. Data Science & Traditional AI/ML
* **Model Selection & Training:** Practical experience training, evaluating, and deploying traditional ML algorithms, including **XGBoost**, **Support Vector Classifiers (SVC)**, and **Linear Regression**.
* **Fine-Tuning:** Experienced in fine-tuning deep learning models (such as **BERT** and domain-specific LLMs) with custom loss functions and hyperparameters.
* **NLP Preprocessing & Feature Engineering:** Implementing text embeddings, data normalization, acronym resolution, synthetic data generation, and custom noise reduction.
* **Data Cleansing with AI:** Utilizing LLMs and AI tools to augment and denoise small or low-quality datasets, achieving significant accuracy gains (e.g., reaching 80% accuracy on corporate PII classification).

### 3. Cloud Architecture & Enterprise Integration (Azure Focus)
* **Azure AI & Cognitive Services:** In-depth implementation of **Azure OpenAI**, **Azure AI Search**, **Azure AI Foundry**, **Azure Language Service (NER)**, and **Azure Document Intelligence (DI)**.
* **Azure Data & Infrastructure:** Architecting enterprise-scale solutions using **Azure Blob Storage**, **Cosmos DB**, **Azure Service Bus**, **SQL Server**, **PostgreSQL**, **Redis**, and **API Management**.
* **Microservices & Orchestration:** 7+ years of experience migrating monolithic legacy architectures to microservices SaaS platforms using **Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS)** and Docker.
* **Distributed Data Pipelines:** Designing robust, real-time message integration systems using **Apache Kafka**, **Azure Service Bus**, and **AWS EventBridge** for high-throughput event processing.

### 4. MLOps, LLMOps & Automation
* **ML Workspace Orchestration:** Managing the full machine learning lifecycle within **Azure ML Workspace**.
* **Pipeline Automation:** Building fully automated pipelines spanning raw data ingestion (Azure Blob Storage) $\rightarrow$ automated preprocessing $\rightarrow$ model training and hyperparameter tuning $\rightarrow$ deployment of managed production endpoints.
* **AutoML & Model Selection:** Utilizing **Azure AutoML** to automate model selection based on performance metrics.
* **CI/CD & CLI Tooling:** Deeply proficient in **Azure CLI**, **Firebase CLI**, **AWS CLI**, and **GitHub CLI** to streamline infrastructure as code (IaC) and deployment pipelines.
* **Model Context Protocol (MCP):** Developing agentic database connections and workflows using the Model Context Protocol to translate natural language into business intelligence queries.

### 5. Traditional Software Development
* **Programming Languages & Backend Frameworks:** Strong delivery experience with **Python** (including **FastAPI**), **C# / .NET (6/8)**, **Node.js**, and **.NET Web API**.
* **Frontend & Mobile:** **JavaScript**, **TypeScript**, **React.js**, **Next.js**, and **React Native** (Android/iOS), including Firebase-backed consumer apps.
* **Application Foundations:** REST API design; **Azure AD SSO** and **Google Auth**; shared middlewares for logging and global exception handling; **Hangfire** background jobs; Domain-Driven Design (DDD) domain services.
* **Domains & Integrations:** Ecommerce/POCs, payment gateways, and financial software; multi-tenant SaaS (clients, teams, roles); third-party social and ads APIs (Meta, X, YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok).

### 6. Stakeholder Management & Consulting
* **RFP & Proposal Preparation:** Experienced in preparing technical proposals, RFP responses, scope documents, and detailed effort estimation models.
* **Architecture Diagrams:** Designing clear, end-to-end system architectures across application, integration, and security layers.
* **Consulting & Leadership:** Proven ability to engage with business, technical, and C-level executive stakeholders to drive AI adoption across diverse domains (including auditing and fintech).
